# Case Study 5: Agile Governance and Team Leadership

## Context
Modern quality leadership requires more than testing oversight. It requires alignment with Agile planning, delivery governance, stakeholder communication, and team capability building.

## Challenge
Teams need quality leaders who can operate across planning, execution, reporting, mentoring, and release decision support.

## Actions Taken
- contributed to PI Planning and OKR alignment
- supported test estimation and delivery planning
- mentored QA team members and improved Agile maturity
- partnered with cross-functional stakeholders including risk and compliance
- strengthened quality governance and reporting visibility

## Leadership value
This work helped connect day-to-day engineering execution with broader business and delivery goals.

## Outcome
The result was stronger alignment across teams, improved quality ownership, and more mature delivery practices in complex enterprise programs.
