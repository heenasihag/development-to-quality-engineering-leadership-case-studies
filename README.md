# Engineering to QE Leadership Case Studies

This repository captures selected case studies from my career journey across software development, automation engineering, and Quality Engineering leadership.

It reflects how I built on a strong software development foundation to design enterprise automation systems, lead quality transformation, improve Agile delivery, and drive measurable business outcomes in banking and enterprise environments.

## What this repository showcases
- progression from software development to QE leadership
- engineering-led automation thinking
- enterprise framework transformation
- shift-left quality strategy
- AI-enabled Quality Engineering
- Agile planning, governance, and team leadership

## Why these case studies matter
I believe strong quality leadership is most effective when built on engineering depth, business understanding, and practical delivery experience. These case studies show how I have combined technical capability with leadership responsibilities to improve quality, speed, and release confidence.
